HOW MONEY WORKS

The Three Balances

Emzini has three distinct balance types — here's what each one means.

Real ZAR

Verified cash deposit

Physical cash you've handed to the admin is recorded here. It doesn't leave this balance until you convert it to App Credits (1:1). It's your on-record proof of real money in the system.

App Credits

Spendable in-app currency

Used to post runner jobs, buy market items, fund bounties, and cover platform fees. New users start with R50 free. You can also add simulated credits to explore the app. Converts 1:1 from Real ZAR.

Cash Float

Runner cash on hand

When a runner collects cash from a delivery customer, that amount is logged here as a float — money the runner is holding on behalf of the platform. It's cleared when the runner hands the cash to admin.
